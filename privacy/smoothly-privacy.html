Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 25, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Smoothly, an iOS app that generates personalized smoothie recipes using AI and computer vision. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Smoothly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our app.

Information We Collect

Camera and Image Data

  • Purpose: We use your device's camera to identify fruits and vegetables for smoothie recipe generation
  • Processing: Images are processed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework
  • Storage: Camera images are not stored or transmitted to our servers
  • Permission: We require camera access, which you can revoke in iOS Settings

Recipe and Usage Data

  • Recipe Information: Ingredients selected, generated recipes, and recipe preferences
  • Usage Patterns: Number of recipes generated per week (for subscription limit enforcement)
  • Storage: Data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase Cloud if you choose cloud backup

Analytics Data (Optional)

  • GDPR Compliant: Analytics collection requires your explicit consent
  • Data Collected:
    • App usage statistics (recipe generation, camera usage, feature interactions)
    • Subscription events (upgrades, renewals)
    • General app performance metrics
  • Provider: Google Firebase Analytics
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics in the app's GDPR consent settings

Subscription Data

  • Provider: RevenueCat handles all subscription processing
  • Data: Subscription status, purchase history, and entitlements
  • Purpose: Managing premium features and usage limits

Technical Data

  • Device Information: iOS version, device model (for compatibility)
  • Network Data: Internet connectivity status for API requests
  • Error Logs: Technical errors for app improvement (no personal data included)

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Process camera images locally to identify ingredients
  • Generate personalized smoothie recipes using AI
  • Store your recipe history and preferences
  • Manage subscription status and usage limits

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features (with consent)
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Enhance recipe generation algorithms

Communication

  • Send important updates about the app (if subscribed to notifications)
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

  • Purpose: Generate smoothie recipes based on identified ingredients
  • Data Shared: Selected ingredients and dietary preferences only
  • Privacy: No camera images or personal data are shared with OpenAI
  • Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

Firebase (Google)

  • Services: Analytics, Cloud Storage, Authentication
  • Data: Usage analytics (with consent), recipe backup (optional)
  • Purpose: App analytics and optional cloud sync
  • Policy: Google Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription management and premium feature access
  • Data: Purchase history, subscription status, device identifiers
  • Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Recipes and preferences are stored securely on your device using iOS data protection
  • Data is not accessible to other apps
  • Local data is deleted when you uninstall the app

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • Recipe backup to Firebase Cloud Firestore (optional feature)
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • You control what gets synced to the cloud

Security Measures

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are securely stored using iOS keychain services
  • No sensitive data is logged or transmitted unnecessarily

Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for analytics at any time

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • In-App: Use GDPR settings to manage analytics consent
  • Email: Contact us at privacy-smoothly@juxhinbakalli.com for data requests
  • Account Deletion: Delete the app to remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Smoothly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Data Retention

Local Data

  • Recipe history is kept until you delete the app or manually clear data
  • Analytics data is retained according to Firebase policies (26 months maximum)

Account Deletion

  • You can request complete account deletion by contacting privacy-smoothly@juxhinbakalli.com
  • We will delete all associated data within 30 days of verification

International Data Transfers

If you are using Smoothly outside the United States:

  • Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses
  • Third-party services (OpenAI, Google, RevenueCat) may process data globally

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

  • Notification: In-app notification for material changes
  • Effective Date: Changes take effect immediately upon posting
  • Version History: Previous versions available upon request
