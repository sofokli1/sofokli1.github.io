Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Introduction

Juxhin Bakalli Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Interval Timer ▸ HIIT & Tabata ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Information We Collect

We designed Interval Timer with your privacy in mind. The App operates with minimal data collection:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Workout Data: Your custom workouts and timer settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, anonymous crash reports may be sent to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We respect your privacy and do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data from other apps
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files
  • Biometric data

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand general usage patterns to enhance user experience

Data Storage and Security

All your workout data and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data that may be transmitted for app improvement purposes.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We carefully select partners who respect user privacy.

Children's Privacy

Interval Timer is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Delete the app and all locally stored data at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the App Store or at our support channels.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable data protection laws including GDPR, CCPA, and Apple's App Store guidelines. We are committed to protecting your privacy rights regardless of your location.
